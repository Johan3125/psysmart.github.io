About us

Psysmart is an organization operating in the field of research and application, consulting and psychotherapy for individuals, organizations and communities.

The organization provides training and psychosocial interventions in the fields of psychology and psychiatry in education and training, consulting and psychotherapy, product marketing.

With specialized skills and knowledge, psysmart will help clients:

  • Listen and learn new perspectives to the problem, current situation
  • Assessment and identification of psychological disorders
  • Provide clients with appropriate and effective treatment
